A little bit about Our business.

My background is in Product Development, Sales and Buying – both in Kildare Ireland and the UK. About 8 years ago I bought a photo frame for my niece Jasmine’s birthday with the intension of placing a photo inside. But instead I decided to print a little verse called “Monday’s Child” with her name and birth details above it, adding little colourful buttons and embellishments. I was showing it to my friends and family and they started to order one here and one there. Then I was asked if I could create personalised framed prints in the form of Wedding frames, Christening frames, Father’s Day frames etc., and really that’s where it all started.
